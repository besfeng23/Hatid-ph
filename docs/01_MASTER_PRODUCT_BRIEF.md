# Hatid Master Product Brief

## Product thesis

Hatid is a Metro Manila-first mobility and delivery platform that may eventually support ride-hailing, padala/package delivery, driver operations, fleet operations, payments, payouts, safety workflows, support/disputes, and AI-assisted recommendations.

## Current reality

Hatid is currently a prototype. It is not a live transport network, wallet, payout system, or dispatch platform.

## Production product modules

1. Rider app
2. Driver app
3. Dispatcher/admin console
4. Merchant/delivery module
5. Fleet/operator module
6. Payment module
7. Ledger/wallet module
8. Driver payout module
9. Support/dispute module
10. Safety/SOS module
11. Promotions/referrals/loyalty
12. Compliance/audit layer
13. Analytics/BI
14. AI recommendations later

## MVP definition

A real Hatid MVP is not a prettier demo. A real MVP must include:

- authenticated rider and driver roles
- verified driver/vehicle eligibility
- real pickup/dropoff geocoding
- real fare quote with expiration
- server-owned trip lifecycle
- basic driver availability
- basic dispatch/offer loop
- trip events/audit trail
- cash payment support with clear receipts
- support ticket workflow
- admin visibility into trips and driver state

## Not MVP

- consumer wallet
- automated payouts
- SpeedCash live integration
- AI dispatch
- surge pricing automation
- fleet self-service at scale
- merchant marketplace
- native mobile apps
- loyalty economy

## Product principle

Ride-hailing is an operations business wearing software. The product must prioritize trust, safety, payment correctness, driver eligibility, support, and auditability over visual polish.
