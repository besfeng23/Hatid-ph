# 17_DATA_RETENTION_POLICY

**Trip Data**

Retention:
7 Years

---

**Financial Data**

Retention:
Permanent

---

**Ledger Entries**

Retention:
Permanent

---

**Audit Logs**

Retention:
Permanent

---

**Compliance Records**

Retention:
According to compliance requirements.

Minimum:
7 Years

---

**Driver Documents**

Retention:
Account Lifetime + Compliance Window

---

**Analytics**

Aggregated:
Permanent

Raw:
Defined archival schedule

---

**Deletion Rules**

Logical deletion preferred.

Audit records never removed.
