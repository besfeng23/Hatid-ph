# 01_BUSINESS_MODEL

**Source Decisions**

*   FD-101 Ride-Hailing + Package Delivery
*   FD-102 Commission-Based Marketplace
*   FD-103 Delivery Included in MVP
*   FD-301 Escrow-Like Marketplace Wallet
*   FD-801 Launch Local, Architect Nationwide

---

**Purpose**

Hatid is a digital mobility and logistics marketplace connecting riders, drivers, and senders through a unified platform.

Hatid does not own vehicles.

Hatid operates as a technology-enabled marketplace.

---

**Revenue Model**

**Primary Revenue**

Commission per completed transaction.

Sources:

*   Ride bookings
*   Package delivery bookings

Formula:

`Revenue = Gross Fare - Driver Earnings`

---

**Secondary Revenue (Future)**

Not part of MVP.

Potential:

*   Fleet subscriptions
*   Priority dispatch
*   Enterprise accounts
*   Advertising
*   Insurance products
*   Financial products

---

**Marketplace Participants**

**Riders**

Consumers booking transportation.

---

**Senders**

Consumers booking package delivery.

---

**Drivers**

Independent service providers.

---

**Hatid**

Marketplace operator.
