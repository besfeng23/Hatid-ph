# **App Name**: Hatid

## Core Features:

- Real-time Ride Matching: Connect riders with available drivers in Metro Manila based on location and preferences using a matching algorithm.
- Automated Fare Calculation: Calculate fares based on distance, time, and demand in Metro Manila, with transparent breakdowns, considering factors like toll fees and rush hour.
- Interactive Map Navigation: Display real-time locations of riders and drivers with animated paths and pins on a stylized map section of Metro Manila. Provides the LLM tool to optimize paths for real-time events like traffic jams or road closures.
- Secure Payment Integration: Enable secure transactions via credit card, e-wallets (GCash, PayMaya), and cash options with clear receipt generation, adhering to Philippine financial regulations.
- Driver Earnings Dashboard: Provide drivers with a dashboard to track earnings, trip history specifically within Metro Manila, and performance metrics.
- Go Online/Offline Toggle: Allow drivers to control their availability for accepting ride requests within Metro Manila.
- Driver Route Navigation: Navigate drivers turn by turn through the streets of Metro Manila, with real-time traffic updates and alternative route suggestions.
- Loyalty Program: Reward frequent riders in Metro Manila with exclusive discounts and perks, such as priority booking during peak hours or discounts on trips to popular destinations.
- Ride Scheduling: Allow users to schedule rides in advance within Metro Manila, ensuring availability for important appointments or events.
- Multi-Language Support: Offer the app in multiple languages, including Tagalog and English, to cater to a diverse user base in Metro Manila.
- Safety Toolkit: Provide safety features such as emergency contact sharing, in-app SOS button linked to local authorities, and real-time trip monitoring for rides within Metro Manila.
- Lost and Found Reporting: Enable riders to report lost items and connect with drivers to retrieve them within Metro Manila.
- In-App Chat: Allow riders and drivers to communicate directly within the app to coordinate pickups and drop-offs within Metro Manila.
- Package Delivery: Enable users to send packages via Hatid, leveraging the existing driver network in Metro Manila, with options for same-day or scheduled delivery.
- Suggested Places: AI-powered tool that will suggest destinations in Metro Manila to the end user based on their search history and local events.
- Emergency Assistance Integration: Integrate with local emergency services (police, ambulance) for quick dispatch in case of accidents or emergencies within Metro Manila.  Includes direct integration with the PNP and local barangay units.
- Accessibility Features: Implement features for users with disabilities, such as voice control, screen reader compatibility, and adjustable font sizes, to ensure inclusivity for all riders in Metro Manila.
- Public Transportation Integration: Provide information and integration with other forms of public transportation in Metro Manila, such as the MRT, LRT, and buses, to offer a comprehensive transportation solution.  This would include real-time arrival and departure information for these services.
- Traffic Incident Reporting: Allow users to report traffic incidents, road closures, and other hazards in real-time, contributing to a community-based traffic monitoring system for Metro Manila.
- Carpooling Option: Introduce a carpooling feature that allows users to share rides with others heading in the same direction within Metro Manila, reducing traffic congestion and promoting eco-friendly transportation.
- Personalized Recommendations: Provide personalized recommendations for drivers and riders based on their preferences and travel patterns within Metro Manila.  This will include preferred routes, driver matching, and ride options.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Sun Yellow (#F7C942) for a vibrant, Filipino-inspired theme.
- Background color: Deep Blue Gradient #00205F → #003D9C
- Accent color: Philippine Red (#D62828) for calls to action and highlights.
- Body and headline font: Rounded, clean, friendly; similar to SF Pro / Inter.
- Consistent spacing and visual hierarchy across all screens, full-rounded pills and cards, and strong smooth shadows (blur 24–40).
- Use clear and friendly icons to represent different features, vehicles, and actions, styled in a modern way and reminiscent of a stylized map of Metro Manila.
- Incorporate micro-animations like glowing pins, breathing CTA buttons, and subtle parallax effects to enhance the user experience, reflecting the energy of Manila.
