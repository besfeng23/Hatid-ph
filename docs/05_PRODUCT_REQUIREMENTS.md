**Acceptance Criteria**

*   Rider can book a ride.
*   Sender can book delivery.
*   Driver can accept jobs.
*   Trip can be tracked.
*   Payment can be collected.
*   Driver earnings calculated.
*   Payouts processed.
*   Support can resolve incidents.
*   Compliance can enforce policies.
*   Audit trail exists for critical actions.
