# 04_COMPLIANCE_REQUIREMENTS

**Source Decisions**

*   FD-401
*   FD-201
*   FD-202

---

**Purpose**

Define minimum compliance standards.

---

**Driver Requirements**

*   Government ID
*   Driver License
*   Selfie Verification
*   Contact Information
*   Emergency Contact

---

**Vehicle Requirements**

*   Vehicle Registration
*   Official Receipt
*   Vehicle Photos
*   Insurance Documents

---

**KYC Requirements**

*   Identity Verification
*   Address Verification
*   Contact Verification
*   Duplicate Account Detection

---

**Verification Process**

1.  Submission
2.  Automated Validation
3.  Manual Review
4.  Approval
5.  Audit Logging

---

**Suspension Conditions**

*   Fraud
*   Document Forgery
*   Unsafe Behavior
*   Repeated Complaints
*   Criminal Activity

---

**Audit Requirements**

*   All compliance actions logged.
*   All approvals logged.
*   All suspensions logged.
*   Immutable audit history required.

---

**Retention Requirements**

*   Compliance documents retained according to policy.
*   Audit records retained permanently.
*   Approval history retained permanently.
